About Artist

Pooja N G

Welcome to my world of graphite and pencils. I'm Pooja N G , a passionate artist who loves working with Pencils.

Ever since I was a child creativity has been my companion. It was during my school years that I first discovered the world of art , and pencil in hand felt like second nature. By the time I reached grade I started drawing figures without much knowledge of the intricate details and strokes required. However over time I noticed improvements in my skills.

Throughout this journey my parents, relatives and friends have been unwavering pillars of support. Their encouragement and belief in my potential have fueled my determination to grow as an artist. They have celebrated my achievements. Stood by me during moments of self doubt laying the foundation for me to develop both skills and confidence.


From those days of sketches until now where I've proudly completed, over 50+ realistic pencil Portraits orders.

"With every pencil's touch, the ordinary transforms into the extraordinary"
