FAQ'S

When will my order be confirmed?
Your order will be confirmed once the payment and order details have been verified, typically within 12 hours.
How can I track the status of my order?
You will receive all tracking details via email once the order shipped.
Is it possible to request a cancellation?
If you need to cancel your pencil sketch order, please contact our customer support promptly. We will assess your request and check the order status. Please be aware that once an order has been processed or shipped, cancellation may not be feasible, but we will make every effort to assist you.
How long does it take to receive my ordered pencil sketch?
The delivery time for your pencil sketch order depends on factors like your location and sketch complexity. We usually process and ship orders within 3-4 business days. Delivery times vary from a few days to a couple of weeks based on your chosen shipping method and location. You'll receive a tracking number upon shipment for monitoring
How do I pay for my order?
To pay for your order, you can use the "Pay with UPI" button, which will redirect you to any UPI-enabled app for payment processing. Alternatively, you can click on "Show QR code" to display a QR code that can be scanned using any UPI app for payment. Please ensure you upload the payment receipt on the order form for confirmation.
